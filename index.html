MY DESIGN PROCESS

The RESEARCH

I determine the root problem and the shortcomings of existing products through researching the market, examining patents, collecting data, and interviewing users.

The Ideas

I discern which ideas are worth pursuing through sketching, brainstorming, prototyping, user testing, and iterating.

The Product

The process culminates in a product that solves the root problem and is intuitive and desired by users.
